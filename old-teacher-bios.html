Michele Fernandes

Michele became a Certified Lead Toddler Teacher & Primary Teacher in 1999. She started her teaching career in a Montessori environment. After 8 years she transferred to working with toddlers in a Public Early Childhood Program. Helping children achieve their full potential and making them feel great about themselves brings a huge smile to Michele's face each and every day. She admires children's endless energy, honesty, creativity, flexibility and resilience which makes every work day different. Throughout her teaching experience she has challenged herself to continue her education at Mass Bay Community College. She currently holds a DEEC Director II qualification. Her hobbies and interests include running, reading and sewing. She spends much of her free time at her summer camp in Maine relaxing at the beach with her husband and 12 year old son.
